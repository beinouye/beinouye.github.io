German Pancakes

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients
Directions
  • 1 TBSP butter (or dairy free alternative)
  • 1 cup milk (or dairy free alternative)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup Flour Blend
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon or Turmeric Spice Mix
  • Optional: canned fruit (drained) or fresh/frozen berries
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Add 1 TBSP of butter to 12" cast iron skillet or 9x13 glass dish and place in oven to pre-heat.
  3. Blend remaining ingredients together in a bowl with an immersion blender and pour into preheated skillet/dish.
  4. Scatter top with fruit if desired.
  5. Bake for 16 minutes.
  6. Use a spatula to remove pancake immediately and set on a plate or tray to cool.
  7. Cut into quarters and serve plain or with butter and syrup.

I love this recipe because it is so easy to make.  My kids like using cookie cutters to cut their pancakes into shapes.

ENJOY!
