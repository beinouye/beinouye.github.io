Pancake Mix

Gluten Free DIY Mixes

Ingredients
Directions
  • 12 cups Flour Blend
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 5 TBSP salt
  • 11 TBSP baking powder
  1. Measure ingredients into a large storage container.
  2. Shake container to combine ingredients.
  3. Set aside for later use.

Make this mix ahead of time.  When it runs out, make it again.  Believe me!  This will save you time and energy later when you are in the mood to bake something quick and easy.

IMPORTANT NOTE:  This mix is intentionally twice as salty as a normal pancake mix would be and should not be used alone.  All of the pancake mix recipes on this site combine this pancake mix with another flour to achieve the appropriate salt ratio.

THIS MIX WILL SAVE YOU TIME AND MONEY!
