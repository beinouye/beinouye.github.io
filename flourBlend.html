Flour Blend

Gluten Free DIY Mixes

Ingredients
Directions
  • 11 cups brown rice flour
  • 5 cups potato starch
  • 3 cups tapioca starch
  1. Measure ingredients into a large storage container.
  2. Shake container to combine ingredients.
  3. Set aside for later use.

This flour blend comes from Carol Fenster's cookbook titled, Wheat-Free Recipes and Menus.  This flour blend is a quadrupled version of "Carol's Rice Flour Blend" found on page 11.

THIS MIX WILL SAVE YOU TIME AND MONEY!
