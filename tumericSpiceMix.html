Tumeric Spice Mix

Gluten Free DIY Mixes

Ingredients
Directions
  • 8 TBSP turmeric
  • 8 TBSP cinnamon
  • 4 TBSP black pepper
  • 2 TBSP ginger
  • 2 TBSP cloves
  1. Measure ingredients into a pint jar or container.
  2. Shake jar to combine ingredients.
  3. Set aside for later use.

This mix boasts anti-inflammatory properties and adds an amazing flavor to recipes.
