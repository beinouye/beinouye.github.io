Fruit Sauce Bread/Muffins

Yield: 1 large loaf, 4 small loaves or 24 muffins

Ingredients
Directions
  • 1 cup fruit sauce
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup Pancake Mix
  • 1/2 cup flax meal
  • 1/2 cup cassava flour
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • Optional add-ins:
    • chocolate chips
    • raisins/dried fruit
    • crushed walnuts
    • crushed pecans
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together fruit sauce, yogurt, sugar, oil, and eggs.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together pancake mix, flax meal, cassava flour and cinnamon.
  4. Stir wet and dry ingredients together until well combined.
  5. Pour batter into greased loaf/muffin pan(s).
  6. Bake loaf for 40-45 minutes. Bake muffins for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Remove pan(s) from oven when a toothpick inserted comes out clean and/or the top bounces back up when touched.
  8. Let loaf cool for 10-15 minutes before removing from pan. Cool loaf completely before slicing. Cool muffins for 5 minutes before removing from pan(s).

Easy. Moist. Delicious. Works with any fruit sauce. I've even used jujube sauce. I like use my cast iron loaf pan.

ENJOY!
