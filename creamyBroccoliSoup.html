Banana Bread/Muffins

Yield: 1 loaf, 4 small loaves or 24 muffins

Ingredients
Directions
  • 1 lb broccoli florets
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 8 oz pack cream cheese
  • 8 oz block cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 bunch green onions
  • 1 bunch cilanto (optional)
  1. Add broccoli, chicken broth, cream of chicken soup, and cream cheese into a large pot or crockpot.
  2. Cook until broccoli is soft.
  3. Add green onions.
  4. Blend ingredients in pot with an immersion blender or upright blender until smooth.
  5. Stir in cheddar cheese and parmesan cheese and let heat until melted.
  6. Serve hot, topped with chopped cilantro if desired.

So easy! So delicious!

ENJOY!
