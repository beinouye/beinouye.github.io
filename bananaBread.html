Banana Bread/Muffins

Yield: 1 loaf, 4 small loaves or 24 muffins

Ingredients
Directions
  • 2 cups mashed banana
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 cup Pancake Mix
  • 1 cup Flour Blend
  • 1 tsp Cinnamon
  • Optional add-ins:
    • chocolate chips
    • raisins
    • crushed walnuts
    • crushed pecans
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together mashed bananas, oil, sugar and eggs.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together pancake mix, flour blend, and cinnamon.
  4. Stir wet and dry ingredients together until well combined.
  5. Pour batter into greased loaf/muffin pan(s).
  6. Bake loaf for 65-70 minutes. Bake muffins for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Remove pan(s) from oven when a skewer inserted comes out clean.
  8. Let loaf cool for 10-15 minutes before removing from pan. Cool loaf completely before slicing. Cool muffins for 5 minutes before removing from pan(s).

I've been working to perfect this recipe for a LONG time. This is my best version so far. I like use my cast iron loaf pan.

ENJOY!
