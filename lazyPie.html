Lazy "Pie"

Yeild: 6 servings

Ingredients
Directions
  • 1 cup milk (or milk alternative)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup Flour Blend
  1. Mix all ingredients together with a fork or blender.
  2. Cook crepes over medium heat on a round skillet.
  3. Cook on each side for about one minute.

These crepes are very versatile. Use as traditional crepes, naan, tortillas, etc.

ENJOY!
