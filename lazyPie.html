Lazy "Pie"

Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients
Directions
  • 1/4 cup butter (or dairy free alternative)
  • 1 cup Flour Blend
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 TBSP baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup milk (or dairy free alternative)
  • 4 cups fruit with juice
  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Heat 9x13 pan or large cast iron skillet in oven with butter until butter is melted.
  3. Mix flour blend, sugar, cinnamon, and baking powder in a bowl.
  4. Whisk egg and milk together in a cup.
  5. Stir liquids into dry mix until batter is smooth.
  6. Pour batter over melted butter in pan and pour fruit with juice evenly over batter.
  7. Bake 25 minutes or until edges start to brown and pull away from the edge of the pan.
  8. Cool for 10 minutes.
  9. Enjoy hot cobbler plain or with a scoop of vanilla icecream (or dairy free alternative).

This recipe is a gluten free version of a favorite family recipe that my dad made for my siblings and I when we were kids and that his mom made for him when he was a kid.

ENJOY!
