Peanut Butter Cookies

Yield: 6 servings

Ingredients
Directions
  • 16 oz jar peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. Mix ingredients together in a mixing bowl. (I prefer to use the dough hook.)
  4. Form dough into balls, roll balls in sugar, and set on baking sheet.
  5. Flatten balls of dough with a fork.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Allow cookies to cool completely on baking sheet. They are very fragile until they cool.
  8. Note: These cookies do not grow any bigger in size while baking.

These are the easiest peanut butter cookies you will ever make!
