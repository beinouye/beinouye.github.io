Simple Recipes for a Gluten Free Diet

Crepes

Yield: 6 crepes

Ingredients
Directions
  • 1 cup milk (or dairy free alternative)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup Flour Blend
  1. Mix all ingredients together with an immersion blender.
  2. Heat round skillet over medium heat.
  3. While lifting and tilting skillet back and forth, pour enough batter to cover bottom of pan.
  4. Flip crepe when a spatula slides easily under crepe.
  5. Cook on second side for about a minute.

These crepes are very versatile. Use as traditional crepes, naan, tortillas, etc.

ENJOY!
